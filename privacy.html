Privacy Policy — The Grand Curator

Last updated: 5 May 2026

This privacy policy describes how The Grand Curator ("the app", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. It applies to the Android app published under the package name com.grandcurator.

What we collect and why

1. An anonymous account identifier

When you first open the app we sign you in to Firebase Authentication using anonymous sign-in. This creates a random user ID (UID) on Google's servers. We use that UID to:

  • save your game progress (gold, relics, restored artifacts, XP, daily streak)
  • post your scores to the global leaderboard
  • record purchases you make in the app (so they can't be replayed)

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal information at sign-in.

2. A "Curator name" you choose

During onboarding you pick a curator name (3–20 characters, letters / digits / underscore only). This name is stored alongside your UID and shown next to your scores on the global leaderboard. Pick a name that doesn't identify you personally — it's visible to other players.

3. Gameplay statistics

We store these on Google Cloud Firestore:

  • gold, relics, scanners, glue counts
  • XP, current streak, total puzzles solved
  • per-artifact fragment progress
  • the date of your last puzzle and last spin

4. Leaderboard entries

When you finish a puzzle, the app sends the puzzle ID, your time, mistake count, and difficulty to a Cloud Function which computes your score and stores the entry under your UID and chosen name. Entries are public (visible on the leaderboard inside the app) but show only your curator name, not your UID.

5. Purchases

If you buy something from the in-app shop, Google Play handles your payment. We never see your card details. We do receive a purchase token from Google which we store to prevent the same purchase being claimed twice and to confirm delivery. We do not link your purchase to anything outside this app.

6. Advertising (AdMob)

The free version of the app shows ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or receive information from your device — including a resettable advertising identifier — to provide and personalise ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy & Terms.

If you purchase the Founder's Pack in-app, all interstitial ads are permanently removed.

7. Crash and integrity

We use Firebase App Check to verify that requests come from a genuine, unmodified copy of the app. This involves Google's Play Integrity service. No personal data is collected by App Check — it only confirms the app hasn't been tampered with.

How information is shared

Game data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Functions, App Check). We do not sell your information and do not share it with third parties other than:

  • Google — provides the Firebase, Play Billing, AdMob, and Play Integrity services that the app relies on.

When you submit a leaderboard score, your curator name and score become publicly visible to anyone using the app's leaderboard screen.

Where information is stored

Firebase services may process and store data in data centres operated by Google in the United States and other countries. By using the app you agree to this transfer.

How long we keep it

We keep your account data for as long as you keep the app installed and using it. If you stop using the app for an extended period, or if you ask us to delete your data, we will remove your users/{uid} document and any leaderboard entries linked to it.

Your rights & data deletion

You can ask us at any time to:

  • tell you what data is associated with your UID
  • delete all data associated with your UID

To exercise these rights, email info@kynto.eu from the device where the app is installed (so we can identify your UID via the Settings screen), or include the UID shown in the app's Settings screen. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also stop all data collection by uninstalling the app. Note that uninstalling does not automatically delete your data from our servers — email us if you want it removed.

Children

The app is rated for ages 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, contact us at the email above and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy we will change the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be announced inside the app the next time you open it.
